Hayao Miyazaki

The man behind a magical universe.

Synopsis

Hayao Miyazaki (宮崎 駿 Miyazaki Hayao, born January 5, 1941) is a Japanese film director, producer, screenwriter, animator, author, and manga artist.
A co-founder of Studio Ghibli, a film and animation studio, he has attained international acclaim as a masterful storyteller and as a maker of anime feature films, and is widely regarded as one of the greatest animation directors.

Miyazaki's Top 10 films

  • 1986 - Castle in the Sky

  • 1992 - Porco Rosso
  • 1997 - Princess Mononoke

  • 2013 - The Wind Rises
